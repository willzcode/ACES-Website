BSC. COMPUTER ENGINEERING

In UENR, our Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering program is designed to provide those passionate about computing, computer hardware and peripheral design as well as software engineering with the requisite tools and skills needed for further advancement in digital technology, computer networking, and computer systems. The depth of our program ensures that students at graduation are at par with contemporaries from similarly placed institutions.
